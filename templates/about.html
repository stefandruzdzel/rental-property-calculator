Welcome to my final project for Harvard's CS50x: Introduction to Computer Science. I was inspired to create this real estate calculator from my own experiences in real estate investing. The existing calculators out there use dumb averages - giving the false impression that one can expect a steady monthly return. The truth is that investing in rental properties can be incredibly volatile: you might be budgetinging for $200/month in maintenance but one month can be issue free, while the next month a sewer line bursts. Additionally, appreciation (and other variables) can vary wildly, causing huge differences in outcomes. I was especially interested in modeling the use of Adjustable Rate Mortgages.


I currently reside in Park City, UT. I have worked in the airline industry for eight years as a self-taught Data Analyst where I built the system that prices flights for Allegiant Air.
